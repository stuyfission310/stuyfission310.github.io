about us

We are Stuy Fission 310, an robotics team from Stuyvesant High School in NYC. Our team, is a dedicated group of students who participate in the FIRST Tech Challenge. We strive not only to excel in our competition, but also to spread the vision of STEM: science, technology, engineering, and math. We document our procedures to assist younger teams in their efforts, and we also do public demonstrations at events such as Maker Faire and the Tribeca Film Festival in the hopes of drawing new people into STEM fields. We do all this to become leaders in the fields of science and technology in the future and to inspire a younger generation to follow our footsteps and surpass our achievements.
